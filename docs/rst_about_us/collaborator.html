There is constant need for specific numerical tools to solve specific problems other than the areas currently implemented within OpenFDEM. If you think the general ideas of OpenFDEM are appropriate for you and your problem, but OpenFDEM lacks the tools needed to address the problem. Please let contact the lead developer Xiaofeng Li <xfli@whrsm.ac.cn> and we would be happy to discuss this further.
